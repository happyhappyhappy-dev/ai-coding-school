# The AI Coding School — Landing Page Brief

## Company
**Name:** The AI Coding School
**What they do:** Connects kids with tutors:
- AI coding tutors
- Coding tutors  
- Math tutors
- Design tutors
- All types of tutors

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Parents of kids (ages ~8-18?)
- **Secondary:** The kids themselves
- **Decision maker:** Parents (pay), but kids need to want it

## Required Features
1. **Calendar widget** — Sign up for trial class
2. **Fun & accessible** — Not corporate/boring
3. **Personality** — Memorable, engaging
4. **Trust signals** — Parents need to trust with their kids

## Vibe / Mood
- Fun but professional
- Tech-forward but approachable
- Kid-friendly but parent-approved
- Energetic but not overwhelming
